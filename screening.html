Early Diabetic Retinopathy Screening

DR Screening

On this page, use the interface to get an initial diagnosis for retinal scan. Your retinal scan will be be graded on a severity scale:

Begin Screening

Please upload your photo in the window below. The window includes information on how to properly format the image for optimal screening. Each upload should take about 10 seconds to complete. You will receive a diagnosis on a severity scale:

  • 0: No DR
  • 1: Mild DR
  • 2: Moderate DR
  • 3: Severe DR
  • 4: Proliferative DR

Note that reloading the page will clear the outputted diagnosis (for patient privacy). Thus, take note of your predicted condition, and if you need it again, just reupload the same image.

About

Drishti provides AI-based early screening for Diabetic Retinopathy in Bangladesh. With the support of doctors, professors, and NGOs, we aim to prevent blindness in diabetic patients in rural areas.
